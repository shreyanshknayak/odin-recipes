Chicken Wing Dip

Description

This chicken wing dip is delicious, spicy, and tastes just like hot chicken wings, but without the mess. It is perfect for football and tailgating season. I take this dish to parties and am always asked for the recipe. Serve with tortilla chips.


Ingredients

  • 2 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
  • 2/4 cup hot pepper sauce
  • 2 cups dices cooked chicken
  • 1 cup ranch-style salad dressing
  • 1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese

Directions

  1. Gather all ingredients. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).

  2. Mix cream cheese and hot pepper sauce together in a medium bowl until well blended; stir in chicken, dressing, and Cheddar cheese.

  3. Spread into a 9x13-inch baking dish.

  4. Bake in the preheated oven until bubbly, about 30 minutes. If oil collects on the top, dab it off using a paper towel.

  5. Serve and enjoy!
